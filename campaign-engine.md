# Campaign Engine

## What a Campaign Is

A targeted outreach to a specific segment of your lead list, with a specific message, at a specific time. That's it.

No automation required at this stage. One person, Airtable, and WhatsApp Business is enough to run effective campaigns at <500 leads.

## When to Run Campaigns

Run a campaign when you have a genuine reason to reach out. Do not manufacture reasons.

**Good reasons:**
- A real seasonal trigger (summer heat, back-to-school, New Year)
- A new product arrived
- A lead went cold 30+ days ago and hasn't bought
- A past customer is likely due for a filter replacement

**Bad reasons:**
- "We haven't emailed in a while"
- A made-up promotion to hit a sales target
- Just because you set up the system and want to use it

## Campaign Types

### 1. Re-engagement Campaign

**Target:** Leads in stage `Contacted` or `Qualified` with no activity in 30+ days
**Frequency:** Monthly
**Goal:** Restart the conversation

**Template (WhatsApp):**
```
Merhaba [Ad], geçen ay su arıtma sistemi hakkında konuşmuştuk.
O zamandan beri fiyatlarımızı güncelledik / yeni bir model geldi.
Hâlâ ilgileniyor musunuz? Kısa bir soru sormak istiyorum.
```

**Why it works:** Personal, short, asks a question. Not a broadcast — feels like a human check-in.

---

### 2. Seasonal Campaign

**Target:** All active leads + past customers
**Frequency:** 3–4x per year (summer, winter, Ramadan/holiday periods)
**Goal:** Create urgency with a real seasonal hook

**Template (WhatsApp — Summer):**
```
Yaz geldi! Bu dönemde çeşme suyundaki klor ve kireç oranı artıyor.
Su arıtma sisteminizi kontrol ettirdiniz mi?
Detaylar için: [Buzsu link]
```

---

### 3. Filter Replacement Reminder

**Target:** Past customers who bought a system 6–12 months ago
**Frequency:** Every 6 months per customer
**Goal:** Repeat revenue from existing customers

**Template (WhatsApp):**
```
Merhaba [Ad]! [Ürün adı] cihazınızı [tarih] tarihinde aldınız.
Filtrelerinizin değişim vakti yaklaşıyor olabilir.
Yardımcı olmamı ister misiniz?
```

**Business value:** This is the highest-ROI campaign type. Customer already trusts you. No acquisition cost.

---

### 4. New Product Announcement

**Target:** Leads with matching product interest
**Frequency:** When genuinely new (don't fake it)
**Goal:** Convert warm leads who were waiting for the right product

## Campaign Execution Process

1. **Open Airtable** → filter to target segment
2. **Export phone numbers** → or work directly from the view
3. **Open WhatsApp Business** → use Broadcast List (not group chat)
4. **Send message** → record send date and count in Campaigns table
5. **Next day:** Record replies received
6. **After 7 days:** Record sales generated

That's the whole process. No tool integration needed yet.

## When to Add Automation

Only automate when:
- You're running campaigns more than 2x/week (unlikely at this stage)
- Manual sending takes >2 hours per campaign
- You have >1000 contacts in your list

Until then, manual is faster to implement and easier to control.

## Metrics That Matter

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Reply Rate | >15% (WhatsApp outperforms email dramatically) |
| Sales per Campaign | ≥ 1 confirmed sale |
| Opt-outs per Campaign | <5% (if higher, reduce frequency) |

## What This Is Not

- Not an email automation sequence
- Not a paid ad retargeting system
- Not a chatbot

## Business Value
Re-engaging existing leads costs nothing. Even a 10% reply rate on 100 cold leads = 10 new conversations = potential sales from people who already know you.

## Implementation Cost
6 hours setup + 2 hours per campaign execution.

## Expected Impact
5–15% re-engagement rate. 1–3 additional sales per campaign for a warm list.

## Priority Score
7/10 — High value, but only after lead capture (Phase 1) and SEO (Phase 2) are running.
