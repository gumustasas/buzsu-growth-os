# SEO Engine

## Strategy in One Sentence

Make Suvesu.com the most trusted Turkish-language resource on water quality, then funnel educated readers to Buzsu.com.tr to buy.

## Why This Works

Water purification is a considered purchase. Buyers research before buying. In Turkey, that research happens on Google in Turkish — and most competitors' content is thin, translated, or keyword-stuffed. A genuine educational authority site with good content will rank.

## The Two-Site Flywheel

```
Suvesu ranks for "su arıtma nasıl çalışır"
        ↓
Reader learns, builds trust in Suvesu
        ↓
Article links to Buzsu product: "Bu cihazı inceleyin →"
        ↓
Reader converts on Buzsu
        ↓
Sale data tells us which keywords convert
        ↓
Write more content on those topics
```

## Content Template for Each Article

Every Suvesu article follows this structure. Do not deviate.

```
H1: [Target query, naturally phrased]
Intro: What problem does this answer? (2 sentences)

H2: [Core educational section 1]
H2: [Core educational section 2]
H2: Buzsu ile Çözüm: [relevant product mention]
   → Link to specific Buzsu product page
H2: Sonuç / Özet

CTA at bottom:
"Evinizin su kalitesini öğrenmek ister misiniz? WhatsApp'tan bize yazın."
→ WhatsApp link
```

## Implementation Steps

1. **Audit first** — Open Google Search Console for Suvesu. Find queries already in positions 5–20. These are the easiest to move to page 1. Start there, not with new topics.

2. **Fix existing pages before writing new ones** — A weak existing article improved to be genuinely useful will rank faster than a new article.

3. **One article per week** — Consistency beats volume. 4 quality articles/month is enough.

4. **Internal links are non-negotiable** — Every Suvesu article must link to at least one Buzsu product page.

5. **Track with Search Console weekly** — Check which new URLs get impressions within 30 days of publishing.

## What Not To Do

| Bad Practice | Why |
|--------------|-----|
| Buy backlinks | Risk of penalty; not needed for this niche |
| Translate English articles | Google detects thin content; Turkish content must be original |
| Target 50+ keywords at once | Focus wins; scatter loses |
| Write for SEO, not readers | Readers who don't trust you won't click through to Buzsu |
| Duplicate content across both sites | Pick one site per topic; Suvesu = education, Buzsu = products |
