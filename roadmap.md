# Roadmap

## Principle

Ship the minimum thing that generates a sale. Add complexity only when a real bottleneck appears.

---

## Phase 1 — Foundation (Weeks 1–2)

**Goal:** First lead captured and followed up within 5 minutes.

| Task | Owner | Time |
|------|-------|------|
| Set up Airtable CRM (4 tables from schema doc) | Solo | 2h |
| Add WhatsApp Business number to Buzsu.com.tr header | Solo | 30m |
| Add "WhatsApp ile Sor" button to every product page | Developer | 2h |
| Create 3 WhatsApp message templates (greeting, product info, closing) | Solo | 1h |
| Connect Buzsu contact form → Airtable via Zapier/Make | Developer | 2h |

**Business value:** Every inbound lead is now tracked. Nothing falls through the cracks.
**Implementation cost:** ~1 day of work + ~$20/month tools
**Expected impact:** 100% lead capture vs. current unknown rate
**Priority score:** 10/10

---

## Phase 2 — SEO Authority (Weeks 3–6)

**Goal:** Rank Suvesu.com for 10 high-intent Turkish water quality queries. Drive organic traffic to Buzsu.

| Task | Owner | Time |
|------|-------|------|
| Audit Google Search Console for existing Suvesu rankings | Solo | 2h |
| Identify 10 target queries (see seo-engine.md) | Solo | 3h |
| Write/improve 10 Suvesu articles targeting those queries | Claude + Solo | 10h |
| Add internal links from each article to relevant Buzsu products | Developer | 2h |
| Submit updated sitemap to Google | Solo | 30m |

**Business value:** Organic traffic compounds. Each article is a permanent asset.
**Implementation cost:** ~15h over 4 weeks
**Expected impact:** 200–500 organic visits/month from Suvesu within 90 days
**Priority score:** 9/10

---

## Phase 3 — Lead Scoring + Prioritization (Week 7)

**Goal:** Sales focus on the 20% of leads most likely to buy.

| Task | Owner | Time |
|------|-------|------|
| Implement scoring rules in Airtable (formula field) | Solo | 2h |
| Add lead source field to all capture points | Developer | 1h |
| Create "Hot Leads" Airtable view (score ≥ 7) | Solo | 30m |

**Business value:** Stops wasting time on cold leads. Hot leads get called first.
**Implementation cost:** 3.5h one-time
**Expected impact:** 2x conversion rate on followed-up leads
**Priority score:** 8/10

---

## Phase 4 — Campaign Engine (Weeks 8–10)

**Goal:** Re-engage cold leads and past customers with targeted WhatsApp messages.

| Task | Owner | Time |
|------|-------|------|
| Segment Airtable by product interest + lead stage | Solo | 2h |
| Write 3 WhatsApp broadcast templates (seasonal, new product, re-engagement) | Solo | 2h |
| Send first broadcast to past inquiries | Solo | 1h |
| Measure reply rate and update templates | Solo | 1h |

**Business value:** Past leads are free inventory. Re-engagement costs almost nothing.
**Implementation cost:** 6h over 3 weeks
**Expected impact:** 5–15% re-engagement rate from cold leads
**Priority score:** 7/10

---

## Phase 5 — KPI Dashboard (Week 11)

**Goal:** Single view of what's working.

| Task | Owner | Time |
|------|-------|------|
| Build Airtable dashboard with 6 core metrics | Solo | 3h |
| Link Google Analytics goal completions manually (weekly review) | Solo | 1h/week |
| Weekly 30-minute review ritual established | Solo | Ongoing |

**Business value:** Stops guessing. Shows which channel generates revenue.
**Implementation cost:** 3h setup + 30m/week
**Expected impact:** Data-driven decisions replace gut feel within 30 days
**Priority score:** 7/10

---

## What's Deliberately Left Out

| Feature | Why Excluded |
|---------|--------------|
| Email drip campaigns | Turkish market responds better to WhatsApp |
| Chatbot automation | Manual follow-up has higher conversion at this volume |
| Paid ad integration | Organic first; add ads only after SEO is profitable |
| Custom analytics platform | Google Analytics + Airtable is sufficient |
| Mobile app | WhatsApp Business covers this completely |

---

## Success Milestones

- **Week 2:** First lead captured in Airtable
- **Week 4:** First sale attributable to the system
- **Week 8:** 50+ leads in pipeline
- **Week 12:** SEO driving 200+ organic visits/month to Suvesu
- **Month 6:** Organic channel generating ≥ 30% of revenue
